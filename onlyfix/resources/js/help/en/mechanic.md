# Help – Mechanic Guide

This guide is for users with the mechanic role. As a mechanic you can manage the problem catalog, handle assigned repair tickets, and track your work.

---

## Dashboard

The mechanic dashboard gives you an overview of your daily workload:

- **Available tickets** – open tickets not yet assigned to anyone
- **My tickets** – active tickets assigned to you
- **In progress** – repairs you are currently working on
- **Completed** – number of finished jobs

The dashboard lists both available and your own tickets so you can immediately see what needs attention.

---

## Managing Tickets

### Browsing available tickets

1. Navigate to **All Tickets**.
2. The list shows all open and active tickets, sorted by priority and date.
3. You can filter by status and priority.

### Accepting a ticket

1. Click on an **Open** ticket.
2. On the detail view, click the **Accept** button.
3. The ticket is assigned to you and its status changes to **Assigned**.

### Starting work

1. Open a ticket you have accepted.
2. Click the **Start Work** button.
3. The ticket status changes to **In Progress**.

### Completing a repair

1. Once the work is done, open the ticket.
2. Click the **Complete** button.
3. The ticket moves to **Completed** status and the customer is notified.

---

## Problem Catalog

The problem catalog contains all possible fault types that customers can choose from when submitting a ticket.

### Adding a new problem

1. Navigate to **Problems**.
2. Click **New Problem**.
3. Enter the name, description, and select a category.
4. Save.

### Editing an existing problem

1. Open the problem list.
2. Click the item you want to edit.
3. Make your changes and save.

**Categories:** Engine, Transmission, Electrical, Brakes, Suspension, Steering, Body, Other.

---

## Statistics

Under the **Statistics** menu you can view:

- **Ticket statistics**: breakdown by status, priority, and time
- **Problem statistics**: most frequent fault types by category

## Tips and FAQ

**Can I see all tickets, not just mine?**
Yes. As a mechanic you can see all submitted tickets, not only your own assigned ones.

**Can I accept multiple tickets at once?**
Yes, you can handle multiple tickets in parallel.

**What if I cannot complete a repair?**
Contact the administrator, who can reassign or reopen the ticket.

**Can I delete a ticket?**
Deleting tickets requires admin privileges.
